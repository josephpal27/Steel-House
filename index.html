Welcome to Steel House

Great Experience for Industrial Solutions

Steel House, Guwahati, established in 1988, is a leading steel trading and distribution firm with over two decades of expertise. Serving North-East India, we offer unbeatable service, competitive prices, and a wide range of products. Our fleet ensures timely delivery, catering to trade and public customers with cost-effective solutions for any enquiry. Focused on eco-friendly practices and socio-economic development, we are committed to providing high-quality products that meet customer needs. With a network of trusted dealers, we strengthen our position by getting closer to customers and expanding our reach.

Our commitment to sustainability and socio-economic development is matched by fast, professional service, a vast inventory, and a deep industry knowledge that keeps us ahead of the competition.
